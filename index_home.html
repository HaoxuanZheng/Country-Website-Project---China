Q&A

How big is China?

China is the largest country in Asia, with a land area of about 3.6 million square miles.

How many ethnic groups are there in China?

China has 56 ethnic groups, 55 minorities and the Han Chinese, who make up 93% of the total population.

Does China have an official language?

The official language of China is Mandarin, meaning universal language.

How long is China's history?

With more than 4,000 years of history, China is one of the oldest countries.

What Is Chinese Cuisine?

Chinese Culinary History

Chinese cuisine is one of the oldest cuisines in the world. It is one of the three major cuisines that have influenced the cuisines throughout East Asia.

Chinese Food Culture

Food is an integral part of Chinese culture and daily life. The Chinese believe that eating good food can enhance family unity and build stronger relationships.

Popular Chinese food by region

Due to differences in geography and climate, each region in China has its own cooking and eating habits.

Sichuan cuisine, Hunan cuisine, Cantonese cuisine, Fujian cuisine, Jiangsu cuisine, Zhejiang cuisine, Anhui cuisine and Shandong cuisine...

Chinese Money

The basic unit of this currency is the "yuan", which has the Chinese symbol "元". 1 yuan is divided into 10 jiao. 1 jiao is further divided into 10 fen.

Origin and history

The earliest items used as currency in China were shells, which sparked the beginning of barter trade. Later, copper coins were created to replace these items.

Conversion

Did you know?

The symbol that represents the yuan (¥) is the same used for the yen, although the pronunciation is different.

The word jiǎo means «horn», while máo means «hair and feathers»

In China is as common to find prices with the symbol ¥ preceding the number as it is to find the character 元 following it. The abbreviation RMB is also used when the symbol ¥ is not available.

More Chinese culture

Chinese civilization has traditionally been considered the dominant culture in East Asia.

China is one of the earliest civilizations in the world, and Chinese culture has had a profound influence on Asia's philosophy, virtues, etiquette and traditions.

What does Chinese culture consist of

Chinese culture includes information on traditions, heritage, art, festivals, language and symbols. Topics include Chinese cuisine, world heritage, Chinese New Year, Kung Fu and Peking Opera.

Flag Of China

The National Flag of the People's Republic of China, also known as the Five Star Red Flag, is a flag with a Chinese red background and five gold stars. The national flag design is a large star with four smaller stars arranged in an arc on the flag.

What do the five stars on the flag represent?

The main star on the Chinese flag represents the Communist Party, while the four smaller stars form a vertical arc, representing the traditional Chinese social classes. The implication is that under the leadership of the Communist Party of China, all these socioeconomic classes are integrated together.
